📢 How to Report a Concern

Reporting is simple — follow these steps to make your voice heard and help the barangay respond quickly:

  1. 🔐 Log in to your account or create one if you don’t have an account yet.
  2. 📊 Click Submit Report (or use Quick in the topbar) to open the report form.
  3. 📝 Fill out the category, location, and a clear description of the issue.
  4. 📷 Attach a photo to help verification (optional but helpful).
  5. 🚀 Click Submit — you’ll receive updates on the dashboard and notifications.

Pro tip: include nearby landmarks or block numbers for faster response.

About

One Heart, One Goal — One Heart, One Goal is more than just a Barangay Complaint and Reporting System — it is a commitment to unity, service, and progress within our community. Built with the vision of strengthening the bond between citizens and barangay officials, this platform provides a simple yet powerful way to voice concerns, share feedback, and take part in shaping a better barangay. In the past, many residents struggled to reach officials or report local issues such as flooding, garbage accumulation, streetlight outages, or damaged roads. One Heart, One Goal addresses this challenge by creating an accessible online space where every citizen’s concern is heard, recorded, and acted upon with transparency and accountability. Our system ensures that every report is properly documented and forwarded to the right department for quick response and resolution. It also allows barangay officials to monitor cases efficiently, track progress, and maintain a clear record of community concerns — helping improve governance, responsiveness, and public trust. Beyond technology, this initiative reflects our shared belief that good governance begins with collaboration. We envision a barangay where people are not just residents but active partners in building a safe, clean, and progressive community. Through One Heart, One Goal, we aim to cultivate a culture of communication, honesty, and teamwork — where every action, big or small, contributes to the betterment of all. Together, we stand by our promise: One Heart, One Goal — Serving the community with integrity, unity, and compassion.
